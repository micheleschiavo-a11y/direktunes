The Solution

Meet The Direktunes Maker (DTM).

DTM is an AI-based assistant for independent musicians who want to publish their music as DT Albums: a digital format for mobile phones — without streaming and without monthly fees.

We pack your album as a native mobile app containing your songs, artwork, lyrics, notes and credits. Fans purchase it once, directly from the App Store, and they will be free to play your music anytime, even when not on wifi.

Available on both Apple App Store and Google Play Store, your music reaches fans worldwide while you keep full control over your catalogue and your earnings.

Your album becomes an app. Fans download it, pay once with Apple Pay or Google Pay, and enjoy your music anytime — no subscription, no algorithm, no middleman.

You set the price. You receive the earnings.

How It Works

Three steps to launch.

DTM handles the app creation. You focus on the music.

1

Submit Your Album

Share your content on DTM and we take care of the rest.

  • Music files (MP3/AAC/WAV)
  • Album cover artwork
  • Lyrics, notes & credits
  • The App icon
2

We Build the App

Our AI automatically creates a polished, native mobile app with your music, artwork, lyrics and full playback controls.

  • iOS & Android apps

  • Professional music player
3

Publish & Earn

We teach you how to publish your app to both stores. Don't worry, it is easier than playing a musical instrument. Once the DT Album is live on Apple App Store and Google Play Store, you will get the links and QR codes that you can share with fans.

  • Google Play Store
  • QR code for live shows
  • Earnings go directly to you
