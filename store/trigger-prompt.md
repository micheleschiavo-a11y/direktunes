# Direktune App Builder — Trigger Prompt

Copy everything between the dashed lines and paste it as your first message in a new Claude Code session opened inside your album project folder (the folder where you placed CLAUDE.md).

---

I want to create my own Direktune album app for both iPhone and Android.

Please read the CLAUDE.md file in this folder — it contains everything you need to build both apps.

Then follow the asset collection protocol in that file: ask me for my music files, artist photo, album artwork, app icon, lyrics, liner notes, credits, and tour dates. Collect everything before writing any code.

Once you have all my assets, build the complete iOS Xcode project and the complete Android Gradle project, run the audit checklist, and then tell me exactly how to upload each app to the App Store and Google Play.

---

## What to prepare before running this prompt

Place all of the following files in your project folder alongside CLAUDE.md:

- Your MP3 files (up to 12, total ≤ 100 MB)
- Your artist/band photo (JPG or PNG)
- Your album artwork (JPG or PNG, square)
- Your app icon (JPG or PNG, square, at least 1024×1024 px)
- Your LRC lyric files if you have synced lyrics (optional)

Claude will ask you for everything else (song titles, album name, notes, credits, tour dates, etc.) during the conversation.
